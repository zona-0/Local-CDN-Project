What is QoS?

Quality of Service (QoS) ensures that important network traffic gets priority and performs well.

It controls delay, speed, and reliability of data transmission.

Good vs Bad Network

✅ Good QoS

  • Low latency
  • High throughput
  • No packet loss
  • Stable jitter

❌ Poor QoS

  • High delay
  • Slow speed
  • Packet drops
  • Unstable connection

QoS in Real Life

📹 Streaming → needs high throughput (no buffering)
🎙️ Video Call → needs low latency & jitter
🎮 Gaming → needs low latency & packet loss

QoS & Network Errors

Poor QoS can lead to network failures:

  • High latency → can cause 504 Gateway Timeout
  • Packet loss → incomplete data transfer
  • Low throughput → slow loading pages
